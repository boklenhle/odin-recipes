Fried Rice

Description

This is a basic fried rice recipe. /you can substitute the shrimp for chicken or pork as well.


Ingredients

  • 3 cups of day-old rice, cooked
  • soy sauce - 3 tablespoons
  • sesame seed oil - 2 tablespoons
  • ginger powder - half teaspoon
  • black pepper
  • oil/butter
  • frozen mixed veggies (usually peas and carrots)
  • 1 egg
  • salt to taste
  • garlic - 1 teaspoon minced or 2 cloves
  • shrimp - 16 oz of raw, detailed, deveined shimp (can be replaced with chicken or pork)

Steps

  1. mix soy sauce, sesame seed oil, pepper, and ginger powder in a small bowl
  2. fry up shrimp (or other protein) in a skillet with butter/oil until cooked. Set aside in a different bowl.
  3. reuse skillet to fry up onion and garlic (don't clean it out)
  4. add frozen veggies to the onion and garlic
  5. add a beat egg to the veggies
  6. after egg and veggies are cooked, add in the rice and soy sauce mixture (you might have to gradually add in the rice)
  7. mix it all up and once it looks cooked, mix in the shrimp (or other protein)
  8. salt to taste
