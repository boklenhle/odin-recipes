Cornbread Casserole

Description

This is Connie's recipe. Probably not fully correct atm. This does notstrong> create enough for the roasting pan - use a double batch for that.


Ingredients

double batch

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic (or a clove fresh)
  • 1 bottle of baby rays BBQ sauce
  • 2 cans of cream style sweet corn
  • 1 cans of regular corn
  • 1 bags (16oz size) of shredded sharp cheddar
  • 2 jiffy cornbread mix (8.5oz)
  • Butter
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Saute the onion and garlic in oil until translucent.
  2. Then add in the ground beef and saute until fully browned.
  3. Drain the grease from the burger as best you can.
  4. Add the BBQ sauce to the ground beef and mix well.
  5. Grease the sides of your pan (the bottom won't need it). Then pour the ground beef and BBQ mix into it (this will make up the bottom of the casserole).
  6. Drain the cans of corn.
  7. Pour the corn (and some salt and pepper) over the ground beef for the second layer.
  8. Do a layer of shredded cheese over the corn.
  9. Mix up the cornbread and pour over everything to create the final layer.
  10. Bake at 350F for about 15-20 min.
  11. After the first 8 min, cut the casserole into slices and put thin slices of butter (like quarter inch thick) into each intersection of slices.
  12. Cook the rest of the way.
