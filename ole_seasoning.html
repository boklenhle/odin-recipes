Ole Seasoning - Taco John's Copycat

Description

This is roughly the seasoning that Taco John's uses on their oles.


Ingredients

  • 4 tsp Lawry's seasoning salt
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • crispy crowns (the potato oles)

Steps

  1. mix it all together put on the cripsy crowns after cooked.
