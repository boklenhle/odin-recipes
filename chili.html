Chili

Description

This is a rough chili recipe. It needs further refined.


Ingredients

  • 15oz diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of mild rotel diced tomatoes and chilis
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 white onion
  • 3 table spoons tomato paste
  • 15oz can kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon granular swerve/regular sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt or 1 teaspoon lite salt
  • 1/4 ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Saute chopped white onion and minced garlic in oil of choice.
  2. Once onion is translucent and soft, add in ground beef. Add salt and pepper over ground beef, then brown.
  3. Once beef is fully browned (no pink left), add in everything else.
  4. Stir until all powders are fully combined in.
  5. Bring to simmer for 15 min or set instant pot to pressure cook for 15 min.

Notes To Self:

If still not hot enough and/or without enough flavor, I could add a jalapeno to the recipe or increase the cayenne pepper. Also if not enough liquid can add a can of tomato juice or tomato soup maybe.
