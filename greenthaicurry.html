Green Thai Curry

Description

This is a creamy curry. I got it off of some Indian recipe website years ago, and modified it for my own liking. I no longer recall which website that was, though.


Ingredients

  • 1 green bell pepper
  • garlic - 2 garlic cloves or 1 teaspoon minced
  • oil - 2 tablespoons
  • salt - 1.25 lite or .75 teaspoon regular
  • ginger - .5 teaspoon fresh ginger
  • green curry paste - 2 tablespoons
  • heavy cream - 2 cups
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • cilantro - 1 tablespoon
  • leeks - 2?
  • rice (to serve it on)

Steps

  1. fry up the chopped up leek, ginger, and bell pepper in oil
  2. mix in salt, pepper, curry paste, and heavy cream
  3. toss in 1 lb of chicken breast and pressure cook for 10 min
  4. serve on bed of rice and add cilantro on top
