Description

This is a recipe my Grandma created when pizza hut stopped selling their cavatini (sometime in the 90s, I think? She was trying to recreate their recipe, and accidentally created this masterpiece of fine cuisine.

This is a sort of half-spicy lasagna cassarole dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 white onion
  • garlic - 2 or three cloves
  • 3 organic mixed mini bell peppers
  • 3.5 cups of pasta
  • 1 jar of bertolli's tomato and basil sauce
  • 14 oz can of diced tomatos
  • 1 jalapeno or 1 can of rotel diced peppers
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 2 bags of cheese (those smaller bags) either mozzarella, pizza blend, or four cheese blend)
  • (optional) mushrooms
  • bag of pepperonis (the smaller bag)

Steps

  1. start cooking the 3.5 cups of pasta (we're going to leave it half uncooked - still a bit crunchy)
  2. brown the burger with onions and peppers while the pasta is cooking (make sure the peppers only cook about half way)
  3. separate and add half the bag of pepperonis to the hamburger mix and stir it all together.
  4. once the pasta is only half way cooked, drain it, and stir in the bertolli, diced tomatos, and rotel/japapeno
  5. combine the pasta and hamburger mixtures into one pot
  6. add generous salt and pepper and stir some more
  7. put it all into a cassarole dish
  8. put the cheese on the very top
  9. bake at 350F for about 25 min or until cheese is bubbling and browned
