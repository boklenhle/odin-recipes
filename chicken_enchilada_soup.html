Chicken Enchilada Soup

Description

This is a chicken enchilaa soup I BSed. It turned out pretty good. I use cream cheese to thicken it, but refried beans can also be used.


Ingredients

double batch

  • 1 chicken broth
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 clove of garlic/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 taco seasoning packet
  • 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 10oz can corn (drained)
  • 1 can rotel diced tomatoes and chilis (drained)
  • 1 8oz block cream cheese/1 can of refried beans for thickening

Steps

  1. Chop the peppers, onion, and garlic
  2. saute the chopped veggies with a pad of butter over medium heat unless translucent/browned
  3. Put the chicken breasts on top of the veggies and pour the broth on top - be sure all the chicken is full covered. Add the toaco seasoning packet. Cook on meat/stew
  4. Add in the beans, corn, and diced tomatoes. Melt in the cream cheese. Pressure cook for 5 min.
  5. juice the lime and dump into the broth afterwards. Mix well.
  6. Serve with fried tortilla strips and monterey jack cheese
