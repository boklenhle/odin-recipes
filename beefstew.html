Beef Stew

Description

This was originally a keto recipe I found back in 2016 online. It appears the original has been taken down, and I've forgotten the original spices, so to make it easy, I just use a store-bought beef stew seasoning packet.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lb of stew meat
  • 1 white onion
  • garlic - 1 teaspoon or 2 cloves
  • two full-sized celery stalks (or one package of the pre-cut snack celery
  • 16 oz baby carrots
  • 1 beef stew seasoning packet
  • (optional) a small bag of those tiny potatoes
  • butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • beef stock
  • (optional) rice

Steps

  1. sautee onions and garlic in butter
  2. add in stew meat and put some salt and pepper on top before browning (maybe like a teaspoon of each)
  3. add in beef stock and seasoning packet, and pressure cook on meat setting
  4. while it's pressure cooking, chop up the veggies and set aside
  5. once it's done pressure cooking, add the veggies and a bit more salt (maybe half a teaspoon) and pressure cook for 7 min.
  6. salt to taste.
  7. (optionally) serve over bed of rice
