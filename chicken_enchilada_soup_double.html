Chicken Enchilada Soup Doubled

Description

This is a chicken enchilaa soup I BSed. It turned out pretty good. I use cream cheese to thicken it, but refried beans can also be used.


Ingredients

double batch

  • 2 chicken broth
  • 2 8oz block cream cheese or 2 cans of refried beans for thickening
  • 1 or 2 white onion
  • 2 clove of garlic/4 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 jalapeno
  • 2 green bell pepper
  • 2 enchilada sauces cans
  • 2 lime
  • 2 lb chicken breast
  • 2 taco seasoning packet
  • 2 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 2 10oz can corn (drained)
  • 2 can rotel diced tomatoes and chilis (drained)

Steps

  1. Chop the peppers, onion, and garlic
  2. saute the chopped veggies with a pad of butter over medium heat unless translucent/browned
  3. Put the chicken breasts on top of the veggies and pour the broth, diced tomatoes and chillis, and enchilada sauce on top - be sure all the chicken is full covered. Add the toaco seasoning packet. Cook on meat/stew
  4. Add in the beans, corn, and diced tomatoes. Melt in the cream cheese. Pressure cook for 5 min.
  5. Juice the limes and dump into the broth afterwards.
  6. Serve with fried tortilla sliced and monterey jack cheese
