Spaghetti

Description

This is good ole fashion sketti.


Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • box of pasta
  • pasta sauce
  • italian seasoning
  • garlic
  • 1 white onion
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. cook pasta
  2. sautee onion and garlic until soft in oil or butter
  3. brown .5-1 lb of ground beef
  4. add pasta sauce and italian seasoning
  5. pressure cook 5 min
  6. salt to taste
