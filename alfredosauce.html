Alfredo Sauce

Description

This is a keto, homemade alfredo sauce recipe.

It's delicious over regular pasta or, if you're on keto, it's fantastic over plain chicken breast.


Ingredients

  • heavy cream - 2 cups
  • butter - 4 oz
  • parmesan - 3/4 cup
  • salt to taste
  • garlic
  • 1 lb chicken breast

Steps

  1. sautee chicken breast with butter and garlic
  2. add in heavy cream
  3. add in the rest of the butter
  4. add in parmesan slowly so it all melts
