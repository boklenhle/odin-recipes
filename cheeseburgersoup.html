Cheeseburger Soup

Description

This is a keto-friendly recipe I originally found on tasty-lowcarb.com. This recipe is tweaked a bit from theirs, though.


Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 16 oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • salt to taste
  • 3 cups beef broth (or just a standard container)
  • 8 oz cream cheese softened
  • 3/4 cup carrots (about two full-sized carrots) grated or finely minced.
  • 3/4 cup celery (about two full-sized stalks) grated or finely minced
  • 1 and 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • a splash of worcestershire sauce (a tablespoon or so maybe?)
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. cook onion until soft and translucent in oil of choice.
  2. brown ground beef with onion.
  3. Add in carrotts and celery and cook until softened
  4. Add in broth and then add in cheeses and stir until melted.
  5. Stir in tomato paste and pepper and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for about 5 min.
  6. Salt to taste and serve with shredded cheese.
