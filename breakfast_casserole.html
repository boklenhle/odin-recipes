Breakfast Casserole

Description

This is a recipe originally found on alldayidreamaboutfood.com

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs
  • 12oz breakfast sausage (cooked and sliced)
  • 1 medium head of broccoli (chopped) OR 12oz bacon (cooked and sliced into bite-sized pieces)
  • 3/4 cup heavywhipping cream
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt OR 1 teaspoon lite salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 8oz shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Grease the interior of a 6 quart slow cooker well (or the inside of an instant pot)
  2. Layer half of the broccoli (or bacon), half of the sausage, and then half of the cheese in the slow cooker/instant pot. Repeat with remaining ingredients in same order.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, whipping cream, garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined. Pour over layered ingredients.
  4. For Slow Cooker: cook on low for 4 to 5 hours or high for 2 to 3 hours, until browned on the edges and set in the center.
  5. For Instant Pot:> set on slow cooker for 5 hours, until browned on the edges and set in the center.
