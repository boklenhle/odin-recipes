Chicken Spaghetti

Description

This is a family recipe my great grandma Fay made up. She was from an era where casseroles were only for poor people. She'd get so mad if you called her chicken spaghetti a casserole, even though that's basically what it is.

  • 1 Chicken (hen or 2 fryers)
  • 1 bell pepper (med)
  • 1 onion (med)
  • 1 can of mushroom soup
  • 1 tomato soup
  • 1 box long spaghetti
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 and 1/2 cheeese (leave some for top)
  • 1 cup celery
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Salt and pepper to taste the chicken. Cook it up.
  2. Strip the chicken from the bones.
  3. Cut up onion, celery. Cook with chicken until tender in chicken broth (7 min).
  4. Cook spaghetti about half way. Drain. Mix soups in with it.
  5. Add all ingredients with spaghetti. (Leave some cheese for the top.)
  6. Pour in buttered baking dish.
  7. Put remainder of cheese on top.
  8. Cook at 400F until boil. Then turn to 350 to keep cheese from burning.
  9. Save broth to pour over for warming.
  10. cover with foil to warm.
