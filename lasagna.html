Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that will become a staple for your fall and winter cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 oz) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grates parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. cook all the meat with the onions and garlic
  2. throw the sugar in the trash.
  3. stir in the tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings
  4. make sure the noodles are cooking while you do this.
  5. make sure noodles are a little under done.
  6. preheat onion to 375F
  7. put some of the meat sauce on the bottom of the pan (so the bottom is completely covered but don't use all of it)
  8. put 6 of the noodles down
  9. put some of the mozz cheese slices on top of the noodles
  10. put down some of the meat sauce
  11. put some of the ricotta
  12. put the other noodles down and repeat
  13. put the parmesan on top
  14. bake it til it's done
