German Apple Cake

Description

This is a delicious German dessert. It's a moist, dense cake that keeps well.

Ingredients

  • 1 lime
  • 1 white onion
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. cut open and de-seed the avocados
  2. use a spoon to get the yummy avocado out of the skin and into a bowl.
  3. cut the lime in half
  4. juice one half of the lime and pour the juice in the bowl with the avocado.
  5. chop up a bit of the whit onion (take 1/8th or so of it, and chop it up pretty fine
  6. (Optional) use the edge of your knife to crush the chopped up onion on the cutting board
  7. put the chopped up onion into the lime and avocado bowl
  8. mix the lime juice, avocado, and onion well
  9. Taste a bit of it. Should taste like lime and avocado with a bit of onion (lol)
  10. Slowly add salt to taste. (I add a dash and mix it well, and then taste it. There will be a point where it stops tasting like a mixture and starts tasting like guac.)
  11. If it tastes too avocado-y, juice the other half of lime and slowly add it in (mixing and tasting) until it tastes right.
