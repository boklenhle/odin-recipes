Butter Chicken

Description

This is a recipe I modified from an idian keto recipe website years ago. Unfortunately, I don't recall which website that was.


Ingredients

  • 14 oz can of diced tomatoes
  • garlic - 5-6 cloves
  • ginger - 1/2 teaspoon minced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • smoked paprika - 1 teaspoon
  • 1 teaspoon salt or double for lite salt
  • garam masala - 2 teaspoons
  • cumin - 1 teaspoon/li>
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 4 oz of butter or coconut oil
  • heavy cream - 4 oz
  • cilantro 1/4-1/2 cup of chopped cilantro
  • cooked white rice (to serve over)

Steps

  1. coat the pot with oil
  2. put the chicken on the bottom of the pot
  3. add all the seasonings (but just 1 teaspoon of the garam masala)
  4. then add in the garlic, ginger, and the diced tomatoes
  5. pressure cook for 10 min
  6. ler naturally release for 10 min
  7. take out the chicken and shred it on a plate
  8. add the cream, butter, and cilantro, and the last teaspoon of garam masala to the mix
  9. mix til cream and butter dissolved and mixed
  10. heat a bit and stir
  11. add chicken back in
  12. serve over a bed of cooked white rice
