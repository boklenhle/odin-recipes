Chicken Rice

Description

This is a chicken and rice filling for burritos. Easy to make and declious.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • chicken taco seasoning
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 chicken stock
  • oil
  • (optional) chicken bullion
  • 1 cup of rice

Steps

  1. cut the chicken breast up into small, bite-sized pieces
  2. chop up the onion and saute it in the oil
  3. pour the chicken stock, taco seasoning, chicken breast cubes, and bullion cube (optional) in with the cooked onion
  4. pressure cook
  5. add rice
  6. pressure cook for 10 min
  7. serve as delicious burrito filling
