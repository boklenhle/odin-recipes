Sticky Ginger Soy Sauce

Description

This is a link to the original recipe


Ingredients

1 Serving Recipe

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 6 cloves garlic (minced) - or 2 tablespoon minced
  • 3 tablespoon fresh ginger
  • 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • small bit of cooking oil

Steps for Sauce

  1. use a fine cheese grater to shred the ginger.
  2. mince the garlic
  3. combine eveerything into a sauce pan
  4. put over low/medium heat until it starts bubbling. Be sure to keep whisking/stirring it. Then remove from heat.
  5. pour over cooked chicken on a bed of rice

Steps for Marinade

  1. use a fine cheese grater to shred the ginger.
  2. mince the garlic
  3. combine eveerything into a sauce pan
  4. put with chicken in bag. Refrigerate for minimum 30 min max a day.
  5. Once you're ready to cook it, coat the bottom of a skillet with a bit of cooking oil (until coated). Cook over medium flame until browned on both sides and cooked all the way through.
  6. Once all the chicken is coated, pour the rest of the sauce into the skillet and bring to a boil. Whisk until the brown bits on the skillet come up and the sauce reduces to a thick glaze.
  7. Add seasame seeds and garnish with green onions, if desired.
