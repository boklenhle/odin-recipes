Refried Beans

Description

I got this recipe off of mexican please's website and slightly modified it

Ingredients

  • lard/butter
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 2 cups of beans
  • 5 cups of water
  • garlic - 1 teaspoon
  • 1 tablespoon of lite salt or 1.5 teaspoons of regular salt

Steps

  1. sautee garlic and onion in lard or butter until done
  2. add beans, water, and salt
  3. pressure cook on beans setting
  4. to refry, add oil to a pan and a ladel or two of the beans and water. Keep the water. This is where all the flavor is.
  5. use a fork or heat-resistant spatula to squish the beans into a paste. Move frequently so it doesn't burn. You may need more of the bean water, if it starts to get crusty and dried out.
  6. serve as a side or use in burritos/tacos
